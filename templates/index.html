Our Story

Experience the vibrant flavors of Tunisia in the heart of the city.

Médina Saveurs brings the authentic taste of Tunisian street food directly to you. Our recipes are passed down through generations, using traditional methods and the freshest ingredients to create delicious dishes that transport you straight to the bustling markets of Tunis.

Founded by Chef Karim in 2015, our restaurant combines the warmth of Mediterranean hospitality with the exciting flavors of Tunisian cuisine. Every dish tells a story of our rich culinary heritage.
